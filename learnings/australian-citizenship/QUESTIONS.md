## Section 1: VALUES Questions (The Dealbreakers)

### Rule of Law

| # | Question | Answer |
|---|----------|--------|
| V1 | Should Australian citizens support and obey Australian law, even if they disagree with a law? | **Yes** — all are equal under the law and must obey it |
| V2 | Should you follow the law even if no one is watching? | **Yes** |
| V3 | Is any person or group above the law in Australia? | **No** — no person, group, or religious rule is above the law |
| V4 | What does the Rule of Law mean? | No person, group, or religious rule is above the law. All Australians are equal under the law |

## Final Tip

**If you're unsure about an answer, apply the values filter:**
- Does the answer protect equality? Pick that one.
- Does the answer put Australian law first? Pick that one.
- Does the answer reject violence? Pick that one.
- Does the answer include everyone? Pick that one.

The values are the compass. When in doubt, follow them.
